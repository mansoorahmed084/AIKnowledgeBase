GenAI Strategy: Beyond the Chatbot

Multi-Directional Strategy
Combines top-down and bottom-up approaches for successful gen AI implementation
1. Strategic Focus
Executives
Focus on high-impact areas, prioritize feasible use cases with high ROI
Managers & ICs
Identify pain points, understand user needs, start with low-risk areas
2. Exploration
Executives
Empower experimentation, foster collaboration and knowledge sharing
Managers & ICs
Experiment with tools, share findings, expand successful initiatives
3. Responsible AI
Executives
Establish ethical guidelines, data governance, content moderation policies
Managers & ICs
Adhere to standards, think proactively about risks, conduct thorough testing
4. Resourcing
Executives
Create data strategy, leverage existing tools, invest in AI talent
Managers & ICs
Leverage team resources, make business case for additional support
5. Impact
Executives
Set clear goals and KPIs, communicate progress to stakeholders
Managers & ICs
Demonstrate tangible impact, align with business goals, track KPIs
6. Continuous Improvement
Executives
Iterative development, regular evaluation, gather feedback mechanisms
Managers & ICs
Continuous testing and refinement, update solutions, continue education

Augmentation vs Automation

Augment:

  • Critical thinking & problem solving
  • Creativity & innovation
  • Relationship building
  • Strategic planning

Automate:

  • Repetitive, rule-based tasks
  • Time-consuming activities
Humans-in-the-loop essential for data prep, prompt design, output evaluation, and monitoring
