Key Takeaways

Edge Computing: AI on devices closer to data source ensures real-time responsiveness, privacy, and offline access. Gemini Nano and LiteRT enable edge deployment.

Solution Needs: Assess scale, customization level, user interaction patterns, privacy requirements, latency, connectivity, accuracy, and explainability before building.

Resources: Understand roles (business leaders, developers, AI practitioners), costs (training, deployment, usage), and time requirements (seconds to months).

Maintenance: Plan for ongoing model monitoring, data updates, software maintenance, and security compliance. Google Cloud's managed environment reduces infrastructure burden.
